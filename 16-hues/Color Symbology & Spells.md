## Spectrum Hues + Symbology Reference ###
Topaz represents MATTER, Amethyst represents TIME, and Citrine represents ENERGY.
Onyx symbolizes VOID, and the brilliant Moonstone LIFE.

Notes
----------
Each color should have an even number of spells, not necessarily including the brush shifts. looks to be about 4 per so far. they should be balanced, relative to each other, in overall usefulness, not power in combat. Spectrum is not just about the power but also about the creatures, the building, the farming, and all the other stuff minecraft is about.  
additionally, maybe brush shifts are scrapped in favor of custom spells for each one. depends on how well I can get the base vfx looking!

an idea for advanced ink slinging - being able to paint your spells. no, not a spell that makes a block change color! imagine this: you swing your brush in an arc. the arc of ink hurls and spreads out and hits every possible block in the arc "drawn" by your mouse movement, and having the spell cast on every block that you looked at while slinging. 
you'd do this by sampling every couple frames while slinging the ink, raycasting to every block hovered over during the swing. send a packet with the set of selected blocks and have an invisible projectile go towards each, for the particle effect to be layered on top. 
on second thought maybe this isn't performant. but how cool would it be? to have a field of freshly planted wheat, painting a smiley face of fully grown wheat with your brush? or, on the flip side - a smiley face of burning wood off of the facade of your friend's base! muahahaha!
