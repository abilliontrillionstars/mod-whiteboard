A joint mod between Laniakea and Stickia that attempts to be a modernized yet unique spin on Astral Sorcery

#### General Progression:
* Players find [[Astral Arcana/Items#Aquamarine|Aquamarines]] and use them to ascend to [[Structures#Naturally Generated|Heaven's Gifts]], to loot [[Astral Arcana/Items#Common Items|Liquid Starlight]] and [[Astral Arcana/Items#Common Items|Celestial Marble]]
