Attunement in [[Main - Astral Arcana]] is binding an object to a [[Lore|Starlight Power]]. The player counts as an object.

## Armor
Only mod specific armor can be attuned to a [[Lore#Constellations|Constellation]]. Attunement grants armor specific abilities based on the Constellation

## Tools
Only mod specific armor can be attuned to a [[Lore#Constellations|Constellation]]. Attunement grants different tools specific abilities based on the Constellation
