## Tools & Armor
Only mod specific armor/tools can be attuned to a [[Lore#Constellations|Constellation]]. Attunement grants different tools specific abilities based on the Constellation. Importantly, since tools and armor are much more simplistic than [[Attunement#Players |Players]]: they lack the [[Attunement#Attunement Trees|Attunement Trees]] players have.

## Players
Players are a special case of Attunement, for instead of a [[Powers#Attribute Changes|basic power]], they can gain more [[Powers#Game Changing|advanced powers]] as well as the basic buffs. For this to be conducted, first the player must build a [[Structures#Player Made|Attunement Sanctum]] to unlock the ability to attune. Once the [[Structures#Player Made|Attunement Sanctum]] is built and use, the player may select a [[Lore#Constellations|Constellation]] to begin attunement.
##### Attunement Trees
For [[Attunement#Players|Player Attunement]], each Constellation hosts a different "skill tree" of sorts, each leaf costs `N` [[Attunement#Leveling|Points]], but grants different [[Powers]]. As well, the powers may either be Major or Minor. [[Powers#Attribute Changes|Minor Powers]] are simplistic attribute changes, such as faster walk speed or less hunger; commonly basic things that are beneficial. While [[Powers#Game Changing|Major Powers]] often change the game in large ways, but with a cost. Said [[Powers#Game Changing|Major Powers]] are found either at the end of a Attunement Tree, or at "choke points" as a gate.

##### Leveling
Once [[Attunement#Players|Attuned]] to a [[Lore#Constellations|Constellation]], the player can gain "levels" with the [[Lore#Constellations|Constellation]]. Each [[Lore#Constellations|Constellation]] has a different method of gaining XP (Ranging from preforming an action, to sacrificing items on the [[Structures#Player Made|Attunement Sanctum]]). And once enough XP is gained, the player levels-up their connection, and gains a "point" (with the next level being marginally more expensive to get). The points then can be spent at the [[Attunement#Attunement Trees|Attunement Tree]] for that specific [[Lore#Constellations|Constellation]].
