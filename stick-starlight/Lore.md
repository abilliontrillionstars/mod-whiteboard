The lore of [[Main - Astral Arcana]] is relatively lose, but contains vague deities and "The Above." Main power is concentrated within the constellations themselves. All of these can be collectively refereed to as `Starlight Power`, or `Starlight Powers` when referring to all. Each Constellation are known for one positive and one negative aspect

## Deities
These are beings of the past that had [[Attunement|attuned]] to a constellation fully; going past the point of no return. While not [[Attunement|Attunable]] directly, deals could possibly be made?

## Constellations
Currently these could be: Abstract Latin Names, Mobs, Mobs chosen by Mythical Creatures, book series 
These are patterns of stars in the night sky that hold immense power within. A compatible object can be [[Attunement|attuned]] to them, ranging from [[Attunement#Armor|Armor]], [[Attunement#Tools|Tools]], and [[Attunement#Players|Players]]. The different constellations are:
* Rabbit, known for speed and fearfulness
* Cow, known for idyllic life and idleness
* Goat, known for willpower and hot-headdedness
* Warden, known for power and single mindedness
* Lord Steve, known for creation and destruction
