Powers in [[Main - Astral Attunement and Artisanry]] are a catch-all term for abilities given to the player. Ranging from basic attribute changes (faster walk, jump height, step up, etc), activated skills gifted by the [[Lore|Constellations]], to damage resistance of certain types



## Attribute Changes
Non-togglable changes, either granted via [[stick-starlight/Items#Tools|Tools]]/[[stick-starlight/Items#Armor|Armor]], or by [[Attunement#Attunement Trees|Attunement Leveling]] 

## Game Changing
Large scales that often a cost to them, granted by [[Attunement#Leveling|Attunement Leveling]] most commonly
