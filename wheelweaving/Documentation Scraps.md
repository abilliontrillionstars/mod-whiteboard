## The Power 
The Power is bound to your soul and persists through reincarnations. This proves a boon through your deaths where one's items may not follow. So long as your skill remains, the Power is available to you.

The Power is like a muscle. Some may be born with more than others, but the real potential is brought through training. You may tire and need recovery, but in time and with diligence, it will grow. It may be overexerted and can cause you injury; treat yourself with care lest the soreness tax you in times of need. 
The gravest injury of this kind that one can face is burning out. Don't forget that the Power is a force far grander than your mortal body. Draw too much and it will burn you right through.
