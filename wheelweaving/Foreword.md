Guidebook entry as a disclaimer for design choices in [[Main - Wheelweaving]]

Hello good channeler, Laniakea here! This entry is a meta note and/or disclaimer to bigger fans of the Wheel of Time series. 

### Disclaimer to the Reader
Making art inspired by a piece of media inevitably invites arguments over interpretation differences. I should admit that I personally have not read the books. I in a way am ghostwriting these mechanics based off the interpretations that my husband, who got me into WoT, relays to me. I am tweaking some things that I deem impractical from a gameplay or balance perspective as I see fit (e.g. "no you can't balefire your friend, how would that even *work* what would *happen* to them"); I understand that these cut corners can rub so people the wrong way - to that, I issue the reminder that I'm doing this all for fun! Please keep feedback constructive and thoughtful. I do this in my (very limited!) free time.

For example, I'm wanting to keep the general vibe but one thing I am shying away from is the sort of gender roles of the power. Gender isn't really a relevant thing in minecraft, including to the player, so I'm reeling back the signal on it.
