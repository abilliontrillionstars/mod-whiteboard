Mod that bestows the One Power from the Wheel of Time book series to the minecraft player. Also adds in-universe paraphernalia like items, or mobs.

## mechanics
- magic is bound to the player
    - no items required to cast once unlocked 
    - predestined per-player power level
        - configurable variance, base, and spread
            - variance is default 0 (uniform among all players)
        - command to set this value for a player
- how are spells (weaves) cast?
    - pick thread combinations?
- two halves of the power
    - Saidar vs Saidin (sai-deen)
    - female vs male (not included ingame)
    - proficient in air+water vs earth+fire
        - spirit is even between
        - note: might make this opt-out
            - configurable to be innate, or
            - have a "Ring of Saidar/Saidin"
    - pick at the start of the mod
    - cannot see vfx from channelers of other half
